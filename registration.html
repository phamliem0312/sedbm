SEDBM 2026 Conference registration fees, policies and key dates

SEDBM 2026 Conference Registration Fees

Registration Type Amount (VND)
PhD Candidate Category 2,000,000 VND
Faculty Category 3,000,000 VND

Registration Policy

The registration fees apply for a maximum of 12 pages only. Additional pages will be charged extra at 500,000 VND per page, up to a maximum of 15 pages.

In case a co-author wishes to attend the conference, it is advisable to register well in advance instead of submitting a spot registration request.

Refund Policy

Should an author wish to cancel a registration for any reason, the conference will refund only 50% of the fee.

Deadline: The last date to submit a registration cancellation request is October 30, 2026.

Conference Fee Includes

  • Admission to Science Sessions
  • Coffee Breaks
  • Lunches
  • Gala Dinner
  • Certificate of Attendance

Key Dates

October 20, 2026 — Registration Deadline
October 30, 2026 — Last date for cancellation request (50% refund)
November 12–14, 2026 — Conference Dates (Hanoi, Vietnam)
