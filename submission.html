Submission & Registration

Everything you need to know about manuscript submission and conference registration at SEDBM2026

Submission guideline

A paper refers to a fully developed manuscript on a scholarly topic. The paper's format follows the guidelines set by Springer Nature Publishing.

Formatting Guidelines: https://beta.springernature.com/pre-submission/submission-guidelines

Authors are invited to submit their manuscripts in English before the deadline via the CMT Platform — use the "Submit Papers" link on this website.

Backup Submission: Manuscripts should also be sent via email: aof_sedbm@hvtc.edu.vn for backup purposes.

SEDBM 2026 Registration Policy

The registration fees apply for a maximum of 12 pages only, additional pages shall be charged an extra fee of 500.000 VND per page up to a maximum of 15 pages only.

In case of co-author wish to attend the conference, they are advised to register in advance rather than requesting on-site registration.

Refund Policy

In case that the author after the registration due to some reason wants to cancel the registration, in such cases, the conference will be able to refund only 50% of the fees only. The last date of application of registration cancellation request submission is October 30th, 2026.

Conference Fee Includes

  • Admission
  • Sessions
  • Coffee Breaks
  • Lunches
  • Gala Dinner
  • A Certificate of Attendance.
