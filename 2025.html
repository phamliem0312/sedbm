About the Conference

A Global Forum for Frontier Market Research

SEDBM2025 is jointly organized by the Academy of Finance, Knowledge Bridge Research Institute, Macquarie University, RMIT University, Nottingham Trent University, Institute of Economics - Ho Chi Minh National Academy of Politics, University of Economics and Law - VNU HCMC, and the University of Greenwich.

The conference brings together world-leading experts in finance, accounting, auditing, economics, and business administration. It offers a collaborative platform for researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to exchange ideas, build partnerships, and discuss contemporary research and practical applications.

Academy of Finance

Founded in 1963, the Academy of Finance (AOF) is one of Vietnam's leading public institutions in higher education and research in economics, finance, accounting, business administration, and related disciplines.

With a strong legacy of academic excellence and innovation, AOF has trained over 100,000 bachelor's graduates, 5,000 master's degree holders, and 500 PhD holders, many of whom hold key positions in government, ministries, and businesses across Vietnam.

The Academy offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in full-time and part-time formats, including international and bilingual pathways. AOF actively supports research, policy consultation, and capacity-building initiatives for the Ministry of Finance and other governmental agencies.

As part of its strategic vision, AOF prioritizes internationalization, research advancement, and digital transformation, while expanding global collaboration with universities and professional bodies.
