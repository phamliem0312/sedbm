About the Conference

Connecting World-Leading Experts

The conference brings together world-leading experts in finance, accounting, auditing, economic and business administration. It serves as a hub for researchers, practitioners and policymakers to connect, share insights and exchange ideas.

SEDBM2026 offers abundant networking opportunities, allowing participants to engage with top scholars and professionals from both academia and industry. The event also provides a vibrant platform for stimulating discussions on contemporary research topics and practical applications.

Academy of Finance

Founded in 1963, the Academy of Finance (AOF) is one of Vietnam's leading public institutions in higher education and research in economics, finance, accounting, business administration, and related disciplines.

With a strong legacy of academic excellence and innovation, AOF has trained over 100,000 bachelor's graduates, 10,000 master's degree holders, and 700 PhD holders, many of whom hold key positions in government, ministries, and businesses across Vietnam.

As part of its strategic vision, AOF prioritizes internationalization, research advancement, and digital transformation. It actively engages in research, policy consultation, and capacity-building initiatives for the Ministry of Finance and other governmental agencies.
