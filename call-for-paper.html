The 9th International Conference on

Sustainable Economic Development and Business Management

SEDBM2026

12th - 14th November 2026 | AOF, Hanoi | Vietnam

Call for Paper

SEDBM - 2026 solicits high quality, unpublished and original contributions that discusses innovative research ideas and innovation and research challenges.

The major focus of our conference are:

  • To share a collaborative knowledge platform among researchers from diversified areas of sustainable economic development, business management, finance and accounting, from different corners of the world
  • To promote a partnership among academia and industry
  • To publicize innovative research ideas and recent trends in sustainable economic development, business management, finance and accounting research
  • To provide a wide platform to the budding researchers for sharing their ideas in front of eminent speakers from highly renowned organizations

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

SUSTAINABLE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT

  • Opportunities and Challenges in Global Frontier Markets
  • National economic growth models
  • Investment and Business Environment.
  • Sustainable Development and the Industrial Revolution 4.0
  • Sharing Economy, Digital Economy, Night-time Economy, Circular Economy

FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

  • Build your Business Resilience in the time of crisis
  • Financial Markets- Growth and Opportunities

  • Financial management in the digital era
  • Fintech; Green Financing

ACCOUNTING AND AUDITING

HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT
