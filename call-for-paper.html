Call for Paper

We invite high-quality, unpublished contributions discussing innovative research ideas

SEDBM 2025 solicits high quality, unpublished and authentic contributions that discuss innovative research ideas and research challenges.

The major focus of our conference is:

  • To share a collaborative knowledge platform among researchers from diversified areas of sustainable economic development, business management, finance and accounting, from different corners of the world
  • To promote a partnership among academia and industry
  • To publicize innovative research ideas and recent trends in sustainable economic development, business management, finance and accounting research
  • To provide a wide platform to the budding researchers for sharing their ideas in front of eminent speakers from highly renowned organizations
